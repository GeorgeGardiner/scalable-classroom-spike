Scalable Classroom Spike

This spike aims to demonstrate the potential benefit of replacing PeerJS and Microsoft IoTHub with a suite of AWS serverless components. Key objectives:

  • Reliability - all connected devices should display no matter what.
  • Scalability - rooms with 20K devices should work as well as rooms with 30 devices.
  • Economy - cost must be less or equal to IoT Hub based equivalent.
  • Security - must be simple and well understood by developers, whilst not compromising privacy and protection afforded by x.509
  • Compatibility - the technology but be accessible to device clients using standard libraries
